Fake News

What is a Fake News??

Fake news is any false or misleading information presented as news and communicated in formats spanning spoken, written, printed, electronic, and digital communication. Fake news can reduce the impact of real news by competing with it. It also has the potential to undermine trust in serious media coverage. Fake news brainwashes people overtime.

Impact of Fake News

  • Financial impacts or impacts on our health
  • Fear
  • Racist ideas
  • Bullying and violence against innocent people
  • Democratic impacts
